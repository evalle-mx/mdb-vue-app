WARNING, How long?

If your organization is in WARNING state, it means we have received at least one failed payment, but your organization has not yet been suspended.
However, if any failed payment reaches the end or period (becomes more than 30 days past due), the following actions will occur:

Project backups will be automatically stopped (they are not deleted). Your Network Access List and Security configurations may be deleted. You will need to pay all outstanding bills in full to resume normal service.

Note: Each billing period runs from the 1st day of the month.

For more details, pleases refer to our FAQ: Billing page.

SUSPENDED, Initial description

Consider that when your account is in SUSPENDED state, it means that your organization has been suspended due to non-payment of invoices, at least one failed payment has more than 30 days past due. Once starting this suspension, Backups are automatically stopped, not terminated, also, Network Access List and Security configuration could be deleted in this period.
To resume normal service, you will need to pay all outstanding bills in full.

In order to remove this SUSPENDED state, your organization Owner or Organization Billing admin must pay any outstanding bills to resume service, once payment is made, this suspension on your organization will be removed.

Note: Each billing period runs from the 1st day of the month.

For more details, pleases refer to our Missed Invoice Payments page.
